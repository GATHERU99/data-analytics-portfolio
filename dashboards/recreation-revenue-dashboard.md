# Recreation Revenue Dashboard

## Objective

Analyze revenue performance across recreation facilities at Village Market.

## Dataset

`datasets/village-market-recreation-data.csv`

## KPIs

- Total Revenue
- Total Visitors
- Revenue by Facility
- Visitors by Facility
- Average Revenue per Visitor

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1: Executive Summary

Visuals:

- Total Revenue KPI Card
- Total Visitors KPI Card
- Revenue by Facility Bar Chart
- Visitors by Facility Bar Chart

### Page 2: Facility Performance

Visuals:

- Facility Revenue Ranking
- Customer Type Breakdown
- Activity Performance Table

### Page 3: Business Recommendations

Focus:

- Best-performing facility
- Underutilized facility
- Revenue growth opportunities

## Expected Insights

- Ozone and Bowling are likely high-revenue facilities.
- Ballpoint has repeat usage potential.
- Family and corporate segments may generate stronger revenue.
