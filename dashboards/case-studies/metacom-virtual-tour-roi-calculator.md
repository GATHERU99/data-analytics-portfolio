# Metacom Virtual Tour ROI Calculator

## Objective

Estimate business impact from implementing virtual tours.

## Inputs

- Monthly Visitors
- Conversion Rate
- Average Booking Value
- Tour Cost

## Outputs

- Additional Leads
- Additional Revenue
- ROI Percentage
- Payback Period

## Formula

ROI = ((Revenue Gain - Cost) / Cost) * 100

## Target Industries

- Hospitality
- Real Estate
- Commercial Spaces
