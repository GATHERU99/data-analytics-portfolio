# Customer Journey Analytics Framework

## Overview

This framework demonstrates how Metacom Virtuals can combine virtual tours and analytics to improve customer experience and conversion rates.

---

## Business Challenge

Most businesses know:

- How many visitors arrive
- How many inquiries they receive

But they do not know:

- Which spaces attract attention
- Where visitors lose interest
- Which sections drive conversions

This creates blind spots in the customer journey.

---

## Solution

Deploy interactive virtual tours with analytics tracking.

Track:

- Entry points
- Time spent per section
- Navigation paths
- Click interactions
- Drop-off points

---

## Example Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|----------|----------|
| Total Visitors | 5,000 |
| Average Tour Duration | 4.5 Minutes |
| Most Viewed Area | Executive Suite |
| Highest Drop-off Area | Pricing Page |
| Inquiry Rate | 7.2% |

---

## Insights

### Finding 1

Executive suites generated the highest engagement.

### Finding 2

Visitors spent 3x longer viewing premium amenities.

### Finding 3

Most users exited before reaching pricing information.

---

## Recommendations

1. Move pricing information earlier.
2. Add booking CTA buttons throughout the tour.
3. Highlight premium spaces more prominently.
4. Reduce navigation friction.

---

## Business Impact

Potential outcomes:

- Higher engagement
- Better lead quality
- Increased booking rates
- Improved customer experience
