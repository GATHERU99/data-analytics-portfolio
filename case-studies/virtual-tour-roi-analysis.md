# Virtual Tour ROI Analysis

## Project Overview

This case study evaluates the potential return on investment (ROI) of implementing immersive virtual tours for hospitality, real estate, and commercial spaces.

## Business Problem

Many businesses rely on static photos and physical visits.

Challenges include:

- Low online engagement
- Limited customer understanding of spaces
- High customer acquisition costs
- Lost leads before physical visits

## Proposed Solution

Interactive virtual tours allowing customers to explore spaces remotely.

Features:

- 360° navigation
- Interactive hotspots
- Analytics tracking
- Lead generation opportunities

## Key Metrics

### Engagement Metrics

- Average Session Duration
- Pages Viewed
- Tour Completion Rate

### Conversion Metrics

- Leads Generated
- Bookings
- Sales Inquiries

### Financial Metrics

- Cost of Tour Development
- Revenue Generated
- ROI %

## Sample ROI Formula

ROI = ((Revenue Generated - Cost of Tour) / Cost of Tour) * 100

## Potential Benefits

- Higher engagement
- Better-qualified leads
- Increased bookings
- Improved customer experience
- Competitive differentiation

- A/B testing
- Customer journey analytics
